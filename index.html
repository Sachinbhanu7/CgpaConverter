How it works?

This converter uses standard formulas accepted by Indian universities to convert your CGPA to percentage:

  • Private Engineering Colleges:
    • For CGPA ≥ 7: Percentage = (CGPA × 7.4) + 12
    • For CGPA < 7: Percentage = (CGPA × 7.1) + 12
  • Mumbai University: Percentage = (CGPA × 7.1) + 11
